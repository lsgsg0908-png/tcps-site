How to Pay with USDT (TRC20)

Follow these steps to complete your payment.

Step 1 — Create Exchange Account

Sign up on Binance, Bithumb, or Upbit.

Step 2 — Buy USDT

Purchase USDT using your local currency.

Step 3 — Select TRC20 Network

Always choose TRC20 when sending USDT.

Step 4 — Copy Payment Address

Copy the address from the payment page.

Step 5 — Send Payment

Send the exact USDT amount.

Step 6 — Access Activated

Your access will be activated automatically.
